# Changelog

Registry-level events — yanks, policy changes, maintainer additions.
Routine aglet/plugin publication lives in `git log` and PR history.

## 0.1.0

Inaugural public registry. Packages are published here by CI from
[`aglet-apps`](https://github.com/agent-rt/aglet-apps) (aglets) and
[`aglet-plugins`](https://github.com/agent-rt/aglet-plugins) (plugins),
each via a reviewed pull request. The `index.json` catalogs are derived
from per-package `meta.json` by the *Regenerate indexes* workflow.
